KD2YYK

My name is Sam Roizen (KD2YYK). I have been a licensed Ham Radio Operator since March 14th, 2022. I am a member of ARES in the NYC-Long Island Section and a member of the New York City Amateur Radio Emergency Communications Service. I frequently participate in Nets on the Limarc Repeater Network and often will be found passing Radiogram Traffic on the Big Apple NTS Traffic Net.

On this website, you will find information about repeaters in NYC and the Tri-State area, nets to look out for, and other resources. The Resources & Docs page holds various resources in the form of links, PDFs, etc...
