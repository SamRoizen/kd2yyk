KD2YYK

My name is Sam Roizen (KD2YYK). I have been a licensed Ham Radio Operator since March 14th, 2022. I am a member of ARES in the NYC-Long Island Section and a member of the New York City Amateur Radio Emergency Communications Service. I frequently participate in Nets and informal conversations on the Limarc Repeater Network and often will be found passing Radiogram Traffic on the Big Apple NTS Traffic Net.



To check out the different repeaters nets I participate in, check out the Repeaters & Nets page. To get in contact with me, check out the Contact page of the website. And to log a contact, you can check out my QRZ Page.
